Kidney Transplantation

Definitive treatment for end-stage kidney disease — living and deceased donor programs, paired exchange & ABO-incompatible protocols.

Transplant

Dialysis (HD & PD)

Hemodialysis and peritoneal dialysis options, vascular access care, and home-based PD programs with training and support.

Dialysis

Kidney Stone Management

Modern shockwave lithotripsy, endourology, and medical stone prevention plans tailored to stone type and anatomy.

Stones

Plasmapheresis (Apheresis)

Plasma exchange therapies for antibody-mediated diseases, transplant desensitization and select autoimmune kidney conditions.

Apheresis

Kidney Biopsy & Imaging

Ultrasound, Doppler, CT and percutaneous renal biopsy for accurate diagnosis and personalized treatment planning.

Diagnostics

Vascular Access Placement

Surgical AV fistula/graft creation, catheter care and troubleshooting for safe, durable dialysis access.

Access

CKD & Hypertension Care

Comprehensive chronic kidney disease clinics — BP control, medication optimization, anemia management and cardiovascular risk reduction.

CKD

Diabetic Nephropathy

Early detection, glycemic control strategies and kidney-protective therapies to slow progression and prevent complications.

Diabetes

Acute Kidney Injury (AKI)

Rapid assessment and supportive care for AKI — fluid management, toxin removal and temporary dialysis if required.

AKI

Pediatric Nephrology

Specialized care for children with kidney disease: congenital anomalies, nephrotic syndrome, UTI and growth & nutrition support.

Pediatrics
