Profile

Dr. Bhavin Brahmbhatt
Consultant Nephrologist & Transplant Physician

Dr. Bhavin Brahmbhatt is an eminent Nephrologist and renowned Kidney Transplant Physician in Indore and Central India. He started and is running a successful living as well as a cadaveric Renal Transplant Program in CHL Hospitals, Indore. He is the Founder and Director of Sovin Kidney Clinic at Indore and also serves as a Consultant Nephrologist and Kidney Transplant Physician in one of the most reputed hospitals in Indore — CHL Hospitals. He has over 20 years of valuable experience in the field of medicine.

Qualifications, Education & Research: Dr. Bhavin Brahmbhatt completed his MBBS in 2000 and MD in Internal Medicine from Government Medical College Baroda, MS University, Baroda, Gujarat in 2004. After specialising, he pursued his super-speciality in Nephrology from Amrita Institute of Medical Sciences, Cochin, Kerala. He is a Diplomate of the National Board in Nephrology and a Member of the National Academy of Medical Sciences.

He is also a member of several prestigious medical societies including the International Society of Nephrology, Indian Society of Nephrology, and the Association of Physicians of India. He is a national speaker in various Nephrology CMEs and conferences, and actively contributes to community service through medical camps and awareness programs. Dr. Bhavin is also involved in scientific research in the field of Nephrology and has published numerous research papers.
